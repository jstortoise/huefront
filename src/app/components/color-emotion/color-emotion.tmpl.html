Color Indices - AVAILABLE FALL 2018

Color is strongly linked to emotional and non-emotional attributes. Research indicates that some of these associations are universal while others are product and cultural sensitive. Designing for a global customer base we need to account for the color connotations we evoke and ensure alignment between the color choice, preferences of the target audience and various contextual (cultural, genderspecific and product related) aspects.

HUEDATA Color Emotion Index (CEI) parses through thousands of research articles comprising the body of research conducted on color, to mine and surface these associations and carefully map them agains colors within the context of demographics, psychographics a product-related attributes when relevant. The CEI is further enriched by our active social listening on Twitter that monitor the popular voice on color and contrast it with research findings for further calibration and validation.

This Members-Only product allows you to benchmark and further modify your color choices against desired attributes to ensure your color massage is indeed on massage!
